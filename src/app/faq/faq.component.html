How does the SkyTrak launch monitor read my golf swing?

SkyTrak is a camera-based Monitor that captures High-speed images of the ball immediately after impact. The images are then used to measure parameters necessary to render shot shape and carry distance.

 What can SkyTrak tell me about my golf swing?

Skytrak Captures and displays accurate flight data including Ball speed, launch angle, side spin, back spin, side angel and more.

 Can I Play in the dark?
